The Travel Square | About

The Travel Square is a Metasearch Engine Technology , Developed To Compare Prices for all Categories and Products of Traveling at a One Stop Place so as to Bring the Entire Global Travel Market at One Binded Platform . It is the Indian Origin Technology Giant in the Field of Travel Which Aims to Build Better Traveling Products , Transparent and Fair Market Products Inclusive of Ideal Customers , Vendors , Associates and Creating Jobs for Affiliate , Connecting the Properties and Travel Business at One Hand . The Company Since June 2014 is Owned and Operated by the Good Vibes Travel Venture Pvt. Ltd. with its Headquarters in Delhi and Corporate Offices Established in Bangalore , Indore , Greater Noida , Noida .

The Travel Square Invests in Travel Technology With a Mission To Connect the Traveling World and Market at One Single Platform to Ensure Better Travelling Experience to Travellers Worldwide .With it's Every Product Distinguishing Itself to a Better Technology in Field of Travelling and Aiming to Create Milestones With Uniqueness in them . Where it Got Started ?

The Travel Square Got Started With an Idea of Bringing More Clarity to The Traveling Business and Making it More Competetive Market Place . Till Now the Technology of Camparision of Hotel Sites, Bikes and Cars Has Largely Taken Over the Current Travelling Sites . It Was an Idea That Sparked The Founders and His Team and Motivated Them To Bring Out Something New in This Field, and That Output was The Travel Square . The Travel Square Was Founded in December 2017 and Got Registered For the First Time in Delhi , India in Dec 2018 After the Popularity of Its First Released Model #TB Hotel .
